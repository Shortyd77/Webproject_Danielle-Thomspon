About the Photographer

Danielle is located in the Whidbey Island area. The love of photography is something she has had since she was little, living in Seattle. She has a wide range of photography styles. Cosplay, street photography, up close and personal photography, and she is open to all creative ideas from her clients. She really has a way of making a picture turn into a beautiful piece of art.
